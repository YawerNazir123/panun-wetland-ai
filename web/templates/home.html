CHATLAM WETLAND

An AI-powered initiative to document, identify, and protect migratory birds of Chatlam Wetland, Kashmir.

Chatlam Wetland: Where Journeys Find Rest

Nestled in the Pampore region of Kashmir, Chatlam Wetland is a quiet refuge for migratory birds that cross continents in search of safety. Here, long journeys pause, life renews itself, and ancient flyways endure—reminding us that protecting Chatlam is protecting the rhythm of nature itself.

Migratory Birds & Seasonal Rhythm

With the onset of winter, distant wings return to Chatlam, guided by ancient routes carved by climate, geography, and instinct. Their arrival marks the changing season and the timeless rhythm of nature that the wetland sustains.
